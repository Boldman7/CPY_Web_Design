Welcome to CPY Coalition

We are the spirit of Corepower Yoga, we have helped to spread wellness through the practice of yoga to hundreds of thousands of students.

We are seeking to be valued for the work we do by our private equity owners, so we can more equitably share in the value we deliver to so many.

I love my Corepower family and students, I just wish Core Power treated me like I was part of that family.

― Kate T, Washington

After 4 years of teaching for CPY, I moved to a new city. When I started teaching for CorePower in my new city, rather than honoring the merit-based pay increases I’d earned over the years, CPY started me at minimum wage. With years of experience, I was making the same as a teacher on her very first day. When I brought this to my manager, she said, ‘I know it’s not fair, but it’s like that for everyone.’ That year, CPY’s private equity owners made hundreds of millions of dollars in profit.

― Kate T, Washington

CPY Business
CPY Business

Corepower Yoga is incredibly lucrative (>50% profit margin) and delivers massive profits to its Private Equity owners (TSG Consumer Partners)

Corepower Yoga pays it's staff < 10%, whereas other service business pay ~30% to staff
Corepower Yoga ensures instructors are replaceable by pushing teacher trainings. As an instructor, you must train your replacement.
Increasing instructor pay to $40 per class would reduce profit for Corepower Yoga's private equity owners only 3%
